Raspberry Glass

A Google Glass-style wearable display powered by a Raspberry Pi.
Completed on March 19th, 2013 Senior Project 2013
This page is still incomplete, so please excuse the sparse amount of details.

For my senior year project, I was inspired by Google Glass.

I bought a MyVu solo, and I had Raspberry Pi lying around, so I put the two together, and now I have a wearable computer of my own.

I played Minecraft while I was presenting my project.

How awesome was that?
