About Me

Inspired by The Setup.

Who are you, and what do you do?

Hi! My name is Adith Radityo, and I am a high school student who likes to experiment with programming and web design when I'm not busy with school stuff.

I like four things: technology, science, minimalism, and indie rock.

You can find me on various social networks by using the links on the left over there.

If you were stuck on a desserted island and you could only have three things with you, what would you bring?

There are just too many variables in that question for me to answer correctly. However, the three things I would most likely bring is 1.) A military-grade laptop or tablet with satellite uplink with a power source powered by either sunlight and/or water, 2.) A matter replicator so I can survive (by eating replicated foods and using replicated tools), and finally 3.) a matter trasporter device (so I could escape the island, of course).

What do you plan to do with your life?

I don't quite know yet. So far my plan is to graduate High School [IN PROGRESS] and graduate college [IN PLANNING] with a major in Nursing and a minor in Computer Science.

In contrast to the lifestyle that the consumerist culture I live in wants me to do (get a job, buy a big giant house, buy stuff, get into debt, work more so you can pay off debt, repeat until death), I plan to get a job that I enjoy doing and pays just enough for me to live my life comfortably in a tiny house that is filled all the latest gadgets I could ever want.
